SARS Import Guidelines

Duty-Free Threshold

Items under R500 are typically duty-free, but still subject to VAT if over R50.

Electronics Import Duty

Arduino boards and electronic components are subject to 15% import duty + VAT.

Documentation Required

Invoice, packing list, and customs declaration. Personal imports under R20,000 have simplified procedures.

Shipping Options

PostNet / Aramex

7-14 days R150-R400 High reliability

Best for small orders. Handles customs clearance.

DHL / FedEx

3-7 days R300-R800 Very high

Fast but expensive. Includes door-to-door service.

SA Post (Registered)

14-30 days R50-R150 Variable

Cheapest but unreliable. Risk of loss or delays.
